Today I woke up excited because I had planned a trip to the beach.

The weather felt perfect the moment I stepped outside.

I packed my bag with sunscreen, a red hat, and my bright blue towel.

When I arrived, the waves were crashing with a steady rhythm.



The sand was warm and felt soft beneath my feet.

I watched children building tiny sandcastles near the shore.

A group of friends played volleyball and their laughter echoed all around.

I sat down and read a mystery novel while the cool breeze brushed past me.

After a while, I took a walk and collected smooth seashells.



Before leaving, I watched the sunset paint the sky in golden shades.
